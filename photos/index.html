Post-Bachelor

Observing at MDM Observatory in Kitt Peak, AZ.


Being an observational astronomer quite often means you will have to write scientific proposals for telescope time to various observatories. My first proposal to get accepted as principal investigator, and it's commonly competitive, awarded me with 7 nights at the 2.4-meter Hiltner Telescope at MDM Observatory in Kitt Peak National Observatory. My daily routine there began at sunset (5:30 PM), observe until sunrise (6:00 AM), then sleep until 3:00 PM. Then, repeat. In general, it is physically and mentally draining to observe at telescopes!

Visit MDM Observatory website to learn more.
